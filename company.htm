Our Commitment

Johnson Landscape Design est. in 1987

We specialise in the creation of exclusive, distinctive, environmentally sustainable landscape architectural and urban design solutions.

We are experienced landscaping consultants, offering a complete garden design and contracting service. We are based in the western suburbs of Perth and service the Perth metropolitan area as well as the surrounding country areas of Western Australia.

Staff Profiles

Mario Johnson Principal, Landscape Architect

Mario earned a degree in Landscape Architecture from Oregon State University in 1974; he joined the firm that year and became a principal in 1985. Mario currently serves as the firm's president and is responsible for operational oversight, ensuring quality and values the collaborative process and brings highly regarded project management skills, imaginative design and detailing to projects of all scales.

As an occassional guest lecturer and design critic at UWA Landscape Architecture Department, Mario contributes to building student knowledge and preparing them for professional practice.

 

Matt Friend Landscape Architect

Matt Friend graduated from the UWA Landscape Architecture program in 2008 and promptly joined Johnson Landscapes. Matt believes that much of the region's landscape is in great need of restoration and that restoration can be undertaken to be both aesthetically pleasing and environmentally sound.

He enjoys revealing ecological processes through the use of graphics and transparent design. Matt spends his free time with Emily, his wife, and James, his baby boy. When he has a chance to get outside, Matt enjoys kite flying, camping, hiking, canoeing/kayaking, snowboarding and exploring Perth and surrounds on foot.

 

Christine Banon
Administrative Staff

Christine joined the administrative team in 2006. Whether she’s answering phones or making sure the office is running smoothly, Christine provides excellent service to customers as well as the Johnson Landscape Design staff, and enjoys helping to make their jobs easier. She makes sure that what needs doing gets done–all in a timely fashion. Christine thinks the Johnson Landscape Design team’s dedication to each job, from start to finish, makes the company special.

She likes to spend time with her husband, 3-year-old son and 2-year-old daughter, playing outside, enjoying games or watching movies.

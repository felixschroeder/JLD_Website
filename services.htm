Gardening Maintanence

Johnson landscapes has a specialized garden maintenance division which is available at a competitive rate. This division was created to serve the needs of the many loyal clients we have accumulated over the years.

Our qualified and experienced horticulturalists are extremely personable and will always pay distinct attention to detail. We can tend the garden you have to the garden you want.

Our maintenance services include:

  • Mowing and weeding
  • Detailed pruning
  • Irrigation maintenance and repairs i.e. watering systems
  • Garden lighting maintenance and repairs
  • Paving cleaning and repairs
